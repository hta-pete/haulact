FAQs

Where do you run?

Our primary regions are Midwest and Southeast. Not West of Denver, No Northeast, No New Jersey, and No Florida. Main Markets are Kansas City, Dallas, Memphis and Chicago.

Where is your terminal?

ACT’s terminal is located in Kansas City, MO.

Do you have drop yards?

Yes. They’re located in E. St. Louis, IL, Olive Branch, MS, Columbus, OH.

What kind of trucks does ACT have?

We run 2015 and newer KW T680s and Freightliner Cascadias.

What kind of trailers?

ACT has over 1,000 dry vans, all being 2014 and newer.

Does ACT allow riders?

Yes. Our free rider policy allows riders on day one, if passenger is over 18.

Does ACT allow pets?

Yes. Our pet policy allows under 40-lb dogs only, with a pet fee.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes. Full benefit plan Insurance is available.

Where are your fuel stops?

Mostly Loves and Pilot. Other options are available based upon regions.

Do you offer training?

Yes, we have training options available, depending on where you live. Please call recruiting for details.
