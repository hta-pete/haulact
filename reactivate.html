Reactivate

RETURN TO TRUCK
DRIVING WITH OUR
QUICK REFRESHER COURSE.

If you’ve been a civilian or a military truck driver before, ACT can get you back on the road in just a few weeks. With our Reactivate Program, we’ll get you up to speed on all of changes that have happened in the industry—from E-Logs to the improved technology.

RETURNING TRUCK DRIVERS

Been off the road for a few years and ready to come back? We’ll help make the return easy, so you can get in on the higher wages available to experienced truck drivers.

  • Eligibility
  • More than 1 year of driving in the past 5 years
  • Have a current Class A CDL

MILITARY TRUCK DRIVERS

Put your military driving experience to work in a lucrative new career at ACT. Our Reactivate Program can help get you on the road fast. And, we are an Approved Veterans Apprenticeship Program.

  • Eligibility
  • Military veteran
  • Valid Class A CDL
  • Heavy-duty truck driving experience
